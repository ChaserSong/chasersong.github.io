CHASER SONG

Singer & Songwriter

MY STORY

My name is Angel Martinez I was born January 6th 1998, in Mexico Federal District. When I turned 4 my mother and father moved to the U.S in pursuit of a better life, leaving me and sister behind. In my early childhood years, I was fascinated by the American singer, Michael Jackson. I was captivated by his songs “Beat It” and “Thriller” because of the way his strong voice and unique dances. I would often replay his videos over and over again until I learned how to imitate his dance moves. Then I’d put on an act for my aunt, uncle, and cousins who took care of me and my sister at the time.

"I'm just a guy trying to play for the world."

In late 2004, my mother and father returned home and by 2005 they decided that the best life they could give me and my sisters was in the U.S. I was scared and didn’t know what to expect. I was leaving behind the only family and friends I knew. Once arriving to the U.S, I struggled with the language and I had trouble making friends in school because I couldn’t communicate. Nevertheless, I pushed through each failed attempt and forced myself to interact with others until one day, it wasn’t difficult anymore.

ORIGINALS

Over the past two years, I have been writing my own lyrics and creating my own melodies. It has been a journey. Sometimes lyrics would just flow and other times I would get stuck. But, I kept pushing and writing. I improve and make changes with each new song.
There are more originals on the way but feel free to have a listen to my released singles.
